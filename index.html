How Vahanam works

1. Vahanam is a global platform where private owners and dealers in countries like the USA, India and more can post their own cars for sale.

2. Guests can freely browse all public car listings with filters and search on the first page; to shortlist or contact sellers, users must create a secure account.

3. After login, users will have a personal dashboard (to be built in backend) to post cars, manage listings, and track enquiries.
